Voting & Rewards

The voting and reward system in Fungibles is designed to encourage community participation, recognize outstanding NFT creators, and provides NFT and the Fungible native token as a reward to participating voters.

Utility

Fungibles leverages blockchain technology to ensure transparency, security, and immutability within its platform. By integrating with a blockchain, Fungibles establishes a decentralized infrastructure that allows for the seamless execution of voting and reward distribution processes.

Supply

Fungibles operates with a predetermined maximum supply of 10 Billion tokens, which means that there is a finite number of tokens that can ever be created.
