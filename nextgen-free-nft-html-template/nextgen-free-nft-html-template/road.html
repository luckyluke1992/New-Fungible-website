2023/2024


Phase 1: PLATFORM FOUNDATION (Q3 2023 - Q1 2024)


- Development of the Fungible platform infrastructure, including blockchain integration, smart contract deployment, and wallet functionality. Basic Website creation.


- User registration and wallet creation process implementation, NFT Showcase feature with submission and curation process.


- Basic voting mechanism allowing users to allocate FUNGE tokens for their preferred NFT collections. Finalise Phase 1 and move to Phase 2.

Phase 2: ENHANCED VOTING AND REWARD SYSTEM (Q2 2024 - Q4 2024)


- Implementation of a weighted voting mechanism, enabling users to allocate different amounts of FUNGE tokens to NFT collections based on their preferences.


- Introduction of a transparent and auditable vote tallying process. Integration of reward distribution functionality for top NFT creators based on voting results. Random NFT reward distribution for participating voters.
